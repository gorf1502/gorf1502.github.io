About

The Buffalo Guitar Duo formed in April of 2008 in Buffalo, New York. The duo consists of David Gorfien and Daniel Stall. Daniel began studying guitar under the direction of Mir Ali and holds a Bachelors Degree in Technology Education from The State University of New York at Oswego. David holds a Bachelors Degree in Music Education from The State University of New York at Fredonia and a Masters Degree in Music Performance from The University at Buffalo. The Buffalo Guitar Duo has performed in venues across the Western New York Region and would love to be considered for your next event.

Performance

The Buffalo Guitar Duo has performed across the Western New York region at venues such as:

  • The Buffalo Zoo
  • The Marriot
  • Holiday Inn
  • The Garrett Club
  • Salvatore's
  • Buffalo Botanical Gardens
